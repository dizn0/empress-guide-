The Split

How shares & the split work
What shares are. Each item is worth a set number of shares (the diamond next to it). The split adds up all the shares and divides them evenly among the players still in the roll. Share values reflect an item's lasting worth, not just current spot price: scrolls hold value as a stable currency, while Diamond prices may drift down over time.

How loot gets handed out. EB / CB / Perfect Pendant are rolled on their own first, and those winners sit out the rest. The share above is each player's point target: going by roll order (highest first), the top roller picks items up to their share, then the next, and so on. Other hosts run it differently; some do a single roll for everything (top rollers take the big items, loot continues down the order), and some skip the tiers entirely and treat every item as equal value to keep distribution simple. Either way, loot distribution is ultimately the host's preferred method, so check how yours runs it.
