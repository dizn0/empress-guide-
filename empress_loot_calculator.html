Note on methods. This uses the separate-roll method: EB / CB / Perfect Pendant are rolled on their own, and those winners sit out the rest. Some hosts instead do one roll for everything, where the top rollers take the big items and the loot continues down the order from the 3rd person on. Either way, loot distribution is ultimately the host's preferred method, so check how yours runs it.
